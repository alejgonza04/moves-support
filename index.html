Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2025

Moves is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We collect and store the following data:

  • Email address and username (via Clerk) – used for account creation and login
  • Bookmarks – saved places you swipe right on
  • Friends list and friend request status
  • Information and IP address (via Clerk for security/session management)

Location Data

Moves uses your current GPS location temporarily to recommend nearby places using the Google Places API. This location data is never stored or saved by us.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To personalize your experience (recommendations, saved places)
  • To allow you to connect with friends and share discoveries
  • To maintain secure sessions and login access (via Clerk)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore (nam5 region) and Clerk authentication services. Only the data you enter or create (e.g., bookmarks, friend lists) is stored.

Your Rights

You can request that your account and all associated data be deleted at any time by contacting us at movessdapp@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out to us at movessdapp@gmail.com.
